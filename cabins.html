The Cabins

Handcrafted shelters designed for deep rest and quiet wonder

Our Philosophy

Built by Hand, Designed for Stillness

Each Limberlost cabin was designed by regional architects who understand the land. Locally milled timber, floor-to-ceiling windows, and thoughtful siting ensure every cabin feels both grounded and open to the landscape. No detail is accidental — from the angle of morning light on your bed to the sound of wind through the screen porch.

Modern comforts are woven seamlessly into the rustic framework: radiant floor heating, rainfall showers, premium bedding, and a fully stocked pantry for morning coffee at your own pace.
