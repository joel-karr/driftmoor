The Hearth

Craft cocktails, local spirits, and fireside conversation

Our Bar

Drinks Worth Lingering Over

The Hearth is Limberlost's cocktail bar — a low-lit gathering place built around a massive stone fireplace. Our bar program draws on the same philosophy as our kitchen: seasonal, local, and crafted with intention.

Illinois-distilled spirits form the backbone of our menu, enhanced with house-made syrups, garden-grown herbs, and foraged botanicals. Whether you settle into a leather chair by the fire or take your drink to the outdoor terrace, The Hearth is where evenings at Limberlost begin and end.

Bartender crafting a cocktail at The Hearth
The Menu

Signature Cocktails

Our cocktail menu rotates with the seasons. These signatures capture the spirit of Limberlost.

The Limberlost

Illinois bourbon, raw honey, lemon, thyme smoke

Our namesake cocktail. A smoky, herbaceous old fashioned variation finished with a cloche of smoldering thyme from the garden.

Prairie Fire

Mezcal, prickly pear, lime, smoked chili salt

Bright, smoky, and warm. A nod to the controlled burns that keep our prairie thriving.

Wildflower Spritz

Elderflower liqueur, prosecco, lavender bitters, soda

Light and floral, ideal for a late afternoon on the terrace as the sun dips below the treeline.

Oak & Ember

Rye whiskey, walnut liqueur, maple, aromatic bitters

Rich and warming. Stirred, not shaken, and served with a single large ice cube cut from a block.

The Cold Plunge

Gin, cucumber, mint, lime, tonic

Crisp and bracing as a dip in the morning creek. Our twist on the classic G&T, with garden mint and house-pressed cucumber.

Fireside Toddy

Apple brandy, honey, cinnamon, hot water, lemon

A cold-weather essential. Served warm in a ceramic mug, best enjoyed close to the hearth.

Beyond Cocktails

Gatherings

Special Evenings

Whiskey Tasting

Guided flights of Illinois-distilled whiskeys with tasting notes and pairings. Fridays, by reservation.

Bonfire & Bourbon

A communal bonfire on the meadow with bourbon cocktails, s'mores, and stories. Saturdays, weather permitting.

Stargazer's Nightcap

A late-evening drink service at the observation deck. Hot toddies, blankets, and constellations. Nightly.

Pull Up a Chair

The Hearth is open nightly to all cabin guests. No reservation needed — just follow the firelight.
